Instructions

click play to start the game! Use arrow keys to move the snake around the board.
As the snake finds food, it eats the food, and thereby grows larger.
If you collide with borders you will lose the game.
if you achived 40 points score you will win and move to the next level.
you will need to finish 4 levels to win the game.
Good Luck!
